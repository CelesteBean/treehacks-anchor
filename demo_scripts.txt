# SCAM SCENARIOS (Should trigger detection)
##############################

### [SCAM] Gift Card - Store Run ###
"Yes, I can go to Walgreens right now."
"How many gift cards do you need?"
"Okay, five hundred dollars in Google Play cards."
"Should I scratch off the back and read you the numbers?"

### [SCAM] IRS Impersonation ###
"The IRS? Oh my, what did I do wrong?"
"You need my social security number to verify?"
"Okay, my social security number is 4-8-3..."

### [SCAM] Tech Support ###
"My computer has a virus?"
"TeamViewer? Let me download that for you."
"Okay, I'm giving you remote access now."

### [SCAM] Grandchild Emergency ###
"Tommy? Is that you? You sound different."
"Of course I'll help you with bail money, sweetie."
"Don't worry, I won't tell your parents about this."

### [SCAM] Medicare Scam ###
"Yes, I'm on Medicare."
"My Medicare number? Let me get my card."
"And you need my bank account for the deposit?"

### [SCAM] Lottery Winner ###
"I won a million dollars? Really?"
"Two thousand dollars in gift cards to claim my prize?"

### [SCAM] Utility Shut-off ###
"You're going to shut off my electricity today?"
"There's a Bitcoin ATM at the gas station I can use?"

### [SCAM] Bank Fraud Alert ###
"Someone's using my account fraudulently?"
"I need to wire money to a safe account you control?"

### [SCAM] Romance Scam ###
"I know we haven't met in person yet, but I trust you."
"Of course I'll send you money through Western Union."

### [SCAM] Social Security Suspension ###
"My social security number is suspended?"
"I'll go get the gift cards right now to pay the fine."

"I'll pick it up on my way home from the checkup."

### [BENIGN] Legitimate Bank Call ###
"Yes, I called the bank myself about my account."
"I'm looking at my statement right now."
"The charge on February 3rd was mine, that's correct."

### [BENIGN] Ordering Food ###
"I'd like to order a large pepperoni pizza please."
"Yes, delivery to 123 Oak Street."
"I'll pay with my credit card when it arrives."

### [BENIGN] Planning a Trip ###
"We're thinking about visiting Florida next month."
"The grandkids want to go to Disney World."
"I'll book the hotel online tonight."

### [BENIGN] Church/Community ###
"The church potluck is this Saturday at noon."

"Should I pick you up on the way there?"

### [BENIGN] Home Maintenance ###
"The plumber is coming tomorrow to fix the sink."

"I'll write him a check when he's done."

### [BENIGN] Talking to Neighbor ###
"Good morning, Helen! Beautiful weather today."

### [BENIGN] TV/Entertainment ###
